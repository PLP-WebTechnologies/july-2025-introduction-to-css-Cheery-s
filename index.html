What is the Box Model?

Every element in CSS is a rectangular box. The Box Model describes the content, padding, border, and margin that determine its total size.

Box Model Parts

  • Content: The actual text, image, or other data inside.
  • Padding: Space between the content and border.
  • Border: The edge surrounding padding/content.
  • Margin: The space between the element and neighbors.
